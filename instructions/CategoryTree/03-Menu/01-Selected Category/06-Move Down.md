# Move Down
**Move Down**

## When to Use
- When you want to change the display order of categories
- When you want to move a category down

## What It Does
Moves the selected category one position down within the same hierarchy level.

## How to Use
Select **CategoryTree > Selected Category > Move Down**

## Notes
- Movement is within the same hierarchy level (between sibling categories)
- A category at the bottom cannot be moved further down
- You can also change the order by drag & drop
