# Enable Dark Display
**Enable Dark Display**

## When to Use
- When you want to switch to a dark color scheme
- When you want to reduce eye strain in dark environments

## What It Does
Switches the entire app to dark mode. A checkmark in the menu indicates dark mode; no checkmark means light mode.

## How to Use
Click GlobalSettings > **Enable Dark Display** to toggle ON/OFF.

You can also toggle it from the command palette with `settings: Enable Dark Display`.

## Notes
- The setting is saved to the project and persists when reopening the project
- This operation is available even in compact mode
