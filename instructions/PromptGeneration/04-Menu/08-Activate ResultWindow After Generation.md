# Activate ResultWindow After Generation

**Activate ResultWindow After Generation**

## When to Use

- When you want the result window to come to the foreground after generation completes
- When you want to work in the background during generation and be notified upon completion

## What It Does

Configures whether the result window is activated (brought to the foreground) after prompt generation completes. When checked, the result window becomes active upon generation completion.

## How to Use

1. Select **PromptGeneration > Activate ResultWindow After Generation** from the menu
2. Toggle with the checkmark on/off

## Notes

- Only effective when "Show ResultWindow After Generation" is enabled
- Even when working in another window, the result window comes to the foreground upon generation completion
- Useful to disable during background continuous generation
