# Copy under Selected

**Copy under Selected**

## When to Use

- When you want to duplicate multiple checked prompts at once
- When you want to consolidate checked prompts as children of the selected prompt

## What It Does

Copies all checked prompts as children of the selected prompt.

## How to Use

1. Check the prompts you want to copy
2. Select the destination prompt
3. Select **PromptTree > Checked > Copy under Selected** from the menu
4. Review the preview in the confirmation dialog and click **Execute**

## Notes

- [Folder Prompt](../../01-Basics/01-Concepts/03-What%20Is%20a%20Prompt.md)s become [Group Prompt](../../01-Basics/01-Concepts/03-What%20Is%20a%20Prompt.md)s, and [File Prompt](../../01-Basics/01-Concepts/03-What%20Is%20a%20Prompt.md)s become [Normal Prompt](../../01-Basics/01-Concepts/03-What%20Is%20a%20Prompt.md)s
- Not available when no prompts are checked
- Not available when no prompt is selected
- Only checked descendants are copied (unchecked descendants are skipped)
- The original prompts remain after copying
