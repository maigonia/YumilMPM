# Check All Leaves
**Check All Leaves**

## When to Use
- When you want to check only leaf prompts rather than prompts with children
- When you want to include only leaf prompts for generation

## What It Does
Checks all prompts that have no children (leaves). Prompts with children are not checked.

## How to Use
1. Select **PromptTree > Check All Leaves** from the menu
2. Or click the "✓ Leaves" button in the toolbar
