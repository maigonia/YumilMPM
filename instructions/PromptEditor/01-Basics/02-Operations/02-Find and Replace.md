# Find and Replace

A feature for searching for text in the editor or replacing it with different text.

## Operation List

| Operation | Menu | Shortcut (Windows) | Shortcut (Mac) |
|-----------|------|---------------------|----------------|
| Find | PromptEditor > Find | Ctrl+F | Cmd+F |
| Replace | PromptEditor > Replace | Ctrl+H | Cmd+H |

## Find & Replace Options

- **Case sensitive** (Aa icon)
- **Whole word match** (Ab icon)
- **Regular expression** (.* icon)
