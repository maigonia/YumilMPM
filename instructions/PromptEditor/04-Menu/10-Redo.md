# Redo

**Redo**

## When to Use

- When you've undone too many operations
- When you want to restore a cancelled operation

## What It Does

Re-applies edits that were cancelled with "Undo". Executing multiple times restores consecutively undone operations.

## How to Use

1. Select **PromptEditor > Redo** from the menu
2. The cancelled edit is restored

## Shortcut

- **Ctrl+Y** (Windows)
- **Cmd+Shift+Z** (Mac)

## Notes

- Making new edits after undoing clears the redo history
- Switching prompts resets the redo history
